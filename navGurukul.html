A Brief Introduction of NavGurukul

Partering with Navgurukul

Navgurukul offers a full-funded skilling program in software engineering with guaranteed job to youth from low-income familes.The program is for one year
and is a residential course

Our students come from diferent parts of the country and the first person to get higher education in their families and communitles,first person to learn
technology driven modern skill and get aspirational jobs. The program and its pedagogy are focused on nourishing changemarkers and free thinkers who

Who are we looking for?


Navgurukul offers a fully-funded skilling program in software engineering with guaranteed job to youth from low-income familes. The program is for one year
and is a residential course.

Our students come from different parts of the countryand are the first person to get higher education in their familes and communities,first person to learn
technology driven modern skills and get aspirational jobs. The program and its pedagogy are focused on nourishing changemakers and free thinkers who

would become the light of change in their communities once they grow professionally.
